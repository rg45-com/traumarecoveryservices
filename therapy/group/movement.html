Reconnect to the body and build self-Esteem

During the warm months we run outdoor movement groups, helping children and adolescents to reconnect to their bodies while building self-esteem, helping develop healthy brains and learning to regulate their nervous systems. Making the body-mind connection is an important step in the process of healing. Parents are welcome to watch these groups.

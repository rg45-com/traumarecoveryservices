Trauma Recovery Services Mission

We provide counseling and assessment to improve the mental health, functioning and well-being of children and adolescents, as well as their families and communities.

Our Values

  • Belief in healing, recovery, resilience and growth
  • Service to our community
  • Ethical and professional actions
  • Excellence and Accountability
  • Joy and play are important
  • Teamwork; we are all in this together!

We believe we can work together to make the world whole again, one child at a time.

Alison C’De Baca - Mental Health Counselor

Alison C’de Baca is a Licensed Mental Health Counselor. She received her M.A. Degree in Counseling from the City University of Seattle and a B.S. in Child and Family Development with a Child Life Specialization from the University of Victoria.

Alison has worked with at risk children, adults and their families for more than 10 years. During this time, she has worked at various levels of care, from subacute inpatient to crisis response in the community. Alison has worked with individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities, children and adolescents with autism as well as those with serious emotional disturbances. She has a strong background in Early Childhood Education and has experience working with infants and toddlers. Alison has extensive experience helping children, adolescents, adults and families with emotional challenges related to trauma, child abuse, violence. Alison also enjoys counseling adults related to challenges that keep them from living their best life.

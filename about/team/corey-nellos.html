{{ page.title }} - Office Manager

Corey Nellos is the office manager here at Trauma Recovery Services. He enjoys spending time with cats, working on his car, fishing, and scuba diving with sharks. He is going to school to be a clinical social worker and plans to help children in need. Corey also likes going racing at the drag strip, listening to 1990’s hip hop, and looking for the next car part to order.

He was born and raised right here in Albuquerque, New Mexico, went to Sandia High school and Graduated in 2014. He is a Juvenile Rheumatoid Arthritis advocate, being diagnosed at age 12. Corey suffers from chronic pain daily, but doesn’t let that dictate his life. He is always trying to find a way to make himself and others smile and laugh.
